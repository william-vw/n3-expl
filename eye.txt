- proof structure:
inference lemma
	r:gives conclusion
	(r:rule rule , r:binding [ r:variable ; r:boundTo ])
	
	r:evidence
		lemma x, y (either inference or extraction)
		a r:Fact; r:gives ..

ignore "useless" inference lemmas:
	r:rule r:because r:source  <http://eulersharp.sourceforge.net/2003/03swap/pass>

extraction lemma
	r:gives conclusion
	r:because (file)
